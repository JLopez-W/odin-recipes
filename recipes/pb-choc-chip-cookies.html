Peanut Butter Chocolate Chip Cookies

Peanut Butter & Chocolate: one of the most irresistible flavor combinations in the world. It’s a match made in dessert heaven. And since cookies are my all-time favorite treat, these ones always hit the spot with their salty-sweet morsels of peanut butter next to creamy milk chocolate. If you’re a fan of peanut butter and chocolate, these cookies are for you.

Preparation

  • About 30 minutes to an hour before you begin, set butter on the counter so it has enough time to soften.

  • Preheat oven to 350°F

  • For this recipe, the baking sheets do not need to be greased, but for more evenly baked cookies and simpler cleanup, line the baking sheets with parchment paper.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter (softened)
  • 1 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 1/2 cups flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2/3 cup creamy peanut butter
  • 6 oz milk chocolate chips
  • 6 oz peanut butter chips

Instructions

  1. In a small bowl, stir together the flour, baking soda, and salt. Set mixture aside.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, use an electric mixer to combine butter, sugar, and peanut butter until creamy.
  3. Mix in the eggs and vanilla.
  4. Slowly add the flour mixture into the large bowl, and mix until well blended.
  5. Stir in the baking chips.
  6. Drop rounded tablespoons of dough onto lined or ungreased cookie sheet.
  7. Bake for 12-15 minutes until edges are golden brown.

Makes about 48 cookies.
